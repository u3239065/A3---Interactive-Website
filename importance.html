over the span of gordon andrews’ influential career, the importance he has left behind is significant. his importance and influence spans multiple disciplines including graphic design, furniture design and industrial design.

pioneer

Within his disciplines, Gordon Andrews was a major pioneer in Australian industrial design, working on mostly everything from furniture design to appliances. His approach within his projects inspired and paved the way for many Australian modernist designers to become influenced through his contemporary techniques.

mentorship

Gordon Andrews was a coveted professional within the design landscape of Australia and was often noted for his extreme but passionate advocacy for good design principles within the education of younger designers. His legacy and mentorship is still seen today through many teachings, publications while his vision is instilled in many institutions and universities to this day.

versatility

Gordon Andrews projects over the years have shown that he is not clamped to a single discipline within design, but was rather versatile in his projects over his decorative career. A great example of his versatility includes his contribution to exhibition design, which showcased Australia’s capabilities on a global scale on how we can express our creativity and technological benefits.
